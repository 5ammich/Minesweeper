Minesweeper Classic

Ah yes, the old classic Minesweeper.
LEFT CLICK on the buttons to dig them up.
RIGHT CLICK on a button to flag it.
Your goal is to get all of the buttons without hitting a bomb (the red ones).
If you get all of them, you win. If you hit a red one, you lose.
Press R to reset the game (this changes the positions of the bombs randomly).
WARNING: There are a lot of bombs (practically a minefield!).
If you can't click anything, the game is over.
